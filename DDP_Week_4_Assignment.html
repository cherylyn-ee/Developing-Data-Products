Best Match College Finder

This app helps you to select the best match college according to criteria below:

  • Outstate: No of outstate students
  • Private: Public/ private college indicator
  • Cost: Personal + Room & Board Costs
  • Top25perc: New students from top 25 % of high school class
  • Apps: Number of applications received

The app then predicts the graduation rate based on this input and displays tab - "Predict Graduation Rate"

College Data

Data used for the application is from "College" data from the ISLR" package. The data contains statistics for a large number of US Colleges from the 1995 issue of US News and World Report. It has 777 observations and 18 variables of which 6 were used - Private, Outstate, Cost (Personal + Room & Board Cost), Top25perc, & Apps.
